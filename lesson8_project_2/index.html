Arthur St.Clair

 

St Clair was a general in the Continental Army. After the Revolution, he came to Westmoreland County and became the wealthiest man in the area.

Nathaniel Greene

 

General Greene was second in command to Washington in the American Army. Greensburg, the county seat of Westmoreland is named after him.
